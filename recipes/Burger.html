Burger

A burger is made of a beef patty, lettuce and some sauces placed inside buns.

Ingredients

  • Grounded beef patty
  • Olive oil
  • Lettuce
  • Sauces such as ketchup, mustard, mayonnaise and etc. As you like.

Steps

  1. In a large bowl, add the beef mince, sauce and combien them.
  2. heat the olive oil in a pan and cook the patty for 10 minutes.
  3. Place a bun on a plate and top it with a patty, lettuce, sauce and another bun.
