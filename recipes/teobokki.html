Teobokki

Teobokki is made of rice cake (The rice cake can also be replaced with flour, which I prefer), chili paste and fish cakes.

According to your preference, you can add some cheese, eggs, other types of noodle and etc.

Ingredients

  • Rice cakes
  • Fish cakes
  • Gochujang (Chili paste)
  • Scallions
  • Soy sauce
  • minced garlic

Steps
  1. Soak the rice cakes in warm water
  2. Mix the sauce in a bowl (Gochujang, sugar, soy sauce, minced garlic)
  3. Boil the mixed sauce in a pot. Once the sauce is boiling, add the race cakes, fish cakes and scallions for 5 minutes.
  4. Ready to eat! ;)
